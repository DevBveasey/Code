Welcome to the Dewey Beach Yacht Club

Come enjoy the day on the beach. Take in the wildlife and relax on the beach. Launch small boats or paddle craft or go for a swim either way the beach is there for our enjoyment. All we ask is that you take all trash with you when you leave.

The club offers Fair priced winter and summer storage for your boats. During the winter safely store in our locked boat yard with our security cameras to protect your summer fun. During the summer we offer inland storage for small boats to give you quick access to the summer fun.

New to sailing? Not a problem the club offers lessons on the boat of your choice from our instructors. All instructors are US Sailing certified and very knowledgeable. Club offers both privet lessons and group lessons.
