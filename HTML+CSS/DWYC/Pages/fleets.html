Welcome to the Dewey Beach Yacht Club Fleets

The sunfish is a boat with its own perks. With its simple design it is easy to rig in under five minutes. Its simplicity make learning to sail it very easy with only two lines! This sail boat is a favorite among young sailors.

The Hobie is a boat load of excitement. This boat offers more room for crew and the excitement of high speeds. If you find yourself a dare devil then this boat is for you, because you can take this to the edge flying a hull out of the water.

Looking to take out the family for a few hours of fun, or a day trip? Then the flying Scott provides the stability and comfort to safely sail with family or store a cooler for a picnic.
