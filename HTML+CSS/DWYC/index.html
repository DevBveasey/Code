Welcome to the Dewey Beach Yacht Club

Need a home away from home then join the club! Spend hours, weeks, even summers down by the bay. The club offers a place like no other with a full kitchen, bath house, and a very large deck. We welcome you to come and Visit us today!

Sailing started as a form of transportation but has evolved into a sport that many have found enjoyable. Not into racing, how about a having a social time out on the calm waters of the Rehoboth bay. Sailing is a form of boating that is about where you are not where you are going.
